# Client Journey Status Guide
**Action-Based Status System: Know Exactly What's Happening & Whose Move It Is**

---

## 🎯 The System at a Glance

Every engagement status answers three questions:
1. **Who are they?** → Prospect (qualifying) or Client (committed)
2. **What just happened?** → Action-based description (Sent SOW, Forms Completed, etc.)
3. **Whose move is it?** → Awaiting Response, Action Required, Delivering, etc.

### Visual Language

- 🔵 **Blue** = Their court → You're waiting (breathe easy)
- 🟢 **Green** = Your court → Needs your attention (take action)
- 🟠 **Orange** = Active work → You're delivering (time blocked)
- ⚪ **Gray** = Complete → Engagement archived

---

## 🗺️ The Complete Journey

### UNIVERSAL START (Everyone Begins Here)

#### Step 1: Send Discovery
**Status:** `Prospect - Sent Discovery - Awaiting Response` 🔵

**What you did:**
- Sent Discovery/Intake Flow to new prospect
- Forms include: Client Information + Intake Questionnaire

**Whose move:** Theirs
**What you're waiting for:** Prospect to complete forms
**Energy state:** Breathe - ball in their court

**How to move forward:** When they submit forms → Status auto-updates to next step

---

#### Step 2: Review Forms
**Status:** `Prospect - Forms Completed - Action Required` 🟢

**What happened:**
- Prospect submitted all discovery forms
- System notified you of completion

**Whose move:** YOURS
**What you need to do:** 
1. Review intake responses
2. Assess fit for your services
3. If good fit → Send consultation link
4. If not good fit → Send polite decline

**Energy state:** Action needed - prioritize this

**How to move forward:** Send consultation link → Update status manually

---

#### Step 3: Send Consultation
**Status:** `Prospect - Sent Consultation Link - Awaiting Booking` 🔵

**What you did:**
- Determined prospect is a good fit
- Sent free consultation calendar link

**Whose move:** Theirs
**What you're waiting for:** Prospect to book time slot
**Energy state:** Breathe - ball in their court

**How to move forward:** When they book → Update status manually

---

#### Step 4: Consultation Scheduled
**Status:** `Prospect - Consultation Booked - Scheduled` 🟠

**What happened:**
- Prospect booked consultation in your calendar
- Meeting confirmed for specific date/time

**Whose move:** Both (prep happening)
**What you need to do:** 
1. Block time for consultation
2. Review their intake forms beforehand
3. Prepare consultation agenda
4. Conduct free consultation

**Energy state:** Active work - consultation delivery

**How to move forward:** After consultation → Determine if Audit or Workshop path

---

### PATH DIVERGENCE (Audit vs Workshop)

After the consultation, you'll know which service to propose. The flow is nearly identical for both paths, just different SOWs and deliverables.

## 🔄 AUDIT PATH

#### Step 5A: Send Audit SOW
**Status:** `Prospect - Sent SOW - Awaiting Signature` 🔵

**What you did:**
- Determined PX Audit is the right service
- Sent Audit Scope of Work for review & signature

**Whose move:** Theirs
**What you're waiting for:** Prospect to review, ask questions (if any), and sign
**Energy state:** Breathe - ball in their court

**Examples of what might happen:**
- They sign immediately → Move to "SOW Signed"
- They have questions → Move to "SOW Reviewed - Action Required"
- They ghost → Follow up after X days

**How to move forward:** 
- If signed → Status auto-updates to "Client - SOW Signed"
- If viewed but not signed → Update to "SOW Reviewed - Action Required"

---

#### Step 5A-ALT: SOW Under Review
**Status:** `Prospect - SOW Reviewed - Action Required` 🟢

**What happened:**
- Prospect opened/viewed the SOW
- May have questions or requested changes
- Haven't signed yet

**Whose move:** YOURS
**What you need to do:**
1. Answer their questions
2. Address any concerns
3. Revise SOW if needed
4. Get signature

**Energy state:** Action needed - respond to move forward

**How to move forward:** After addressing questions → Back to "Awaiting Signature" or straight to "SOW Signed"

---

#### Step 6A: SOW Signed
**Status:** `Client - SOW Signed - Action Required` 🟢

**What happened:**
- Prospect signed the SOW
- They're now officially a CLIENT
- Commitment made, payment pending

**Whose move:** YOURS
**What you need to do:**
1. Generate invoice in system
2. Create Stripe payment link
3. Send invoice to client

**Energy state:** Action needed - generate invoice

**Example invoice:**
- PX Audit: $8,500
- Due upon receipt
- Stripe link included

**How to move forward:** Send invoice → Update to "Sent Invoice - Awaiting Payment"

---

#### Step 7A: Invoice Sent
**Status:** `Client - Sent Invoice - Awaiting Payment` 🔵

**What you did:**
- Generated invoice
- Sent Stripe payment link to client

**Whose move:** Theirs
**What you're waiting for:** Payment confirmation from Stripe
**Energy state:** Breathe - ball in their court

**How to move forward:** Stripe payment received → Status auto-updates to "Delivering Audit"

---

#### Step 8A: Audit Delivery
**Status:** `Client - Payment Received - Delivering Audit` 🟠

**What happened:**
- Client paid invoice via Stripe
- Payment confirmed
- Work officially begins

**Whose move:** YOURS (Active delivery)
**What you're doing:**
1. Conduct comprehensive PX audit
2. Document findings
3. Create recommendations
4. Prepare final report
5. Prepare presentation if included

**Energy state:** Active work - delivering value

**Timeline:** Typically 2-3 weeks

**How to move forward:** When deliverables ready → Update to "Audit Delivered - Awaiting Feedback"

---

#### Step 9A: Audit Delivered
**Status:** `Client - Audit Delivered - Awaiting Feedback` 🔵

**What you did:**
- Completed comprehensive audit
- Delivered final report + recommendations
- Sent all materials to client

**Whose move:** Theirs
**What you're waiting for:** Client to review deliverables and provide feedback
**Energy state:** Breathe - ball in their court

**What might happen:**
- They love it → Move to "Complete - Archived"
- They have questions → Move to "Feedback Received - Action Required"
- They want revisions → Move to "Feedback Received - Action Required"

**How to move forward:**
- If no feedback needed → Update to "Complete - Archived"
- If feedback received → Update to "Feedback Received - Action Required"

---

#### Step 9A-ALT: Address Feedback
**Status:** `Client - Feedback Received - Action Required` 🟢

**What happened:**
- Client reviewed deliverables
- Provided feedback, questions, or revision requests

**Whose move:** YOURS
**What you need to do:**
1. Review their feedback
2. Answer questions
3. Make revisions if reasonable
4. Re-deliver updated materials

**Energy state:** Action needed - finalize engagement

**How to move forward:** After addressing feedback → Update to "Complete - Archived"

---

#### Step 10A: Complete
**Status:** `Client - Engagement Complete - Archived` ⚪

**What happened:**
- Audit delivered
- All feedback addressed
- Client satisfied
- Engagement officially closed

**Whose move:** No one (archived)
**What this means:**
- Historical record maintained
- Available for reference
- Track for future opportunities
- Include in success metrics

**Energy state:** Complete - track for nurture

**How to move forward:** Consider adding to nurture sequence for future projects

## 🔍 Quick Reference: What Do I Do When...

### "I just got a new inquiry"
1. Create Draft Engagement
2. Send Discovery Flow
3. Status: `Prospect - Sent Discovery - Awaiting Response` 🔵
4. Wait for them to complete forms

### "They completed the intake forms"
1. Review their responses
2. Status: `Prospect - Forms Completed - Action Required` 🟢
3. Assess fit
4. If good fit → Send consultation link
5. If not → Send polite decline

### "They booked a consultation"
1. Status: `Prospect - Consultation Booked - Scheduled` 🟠
2. Review intake before meeting
3. Conduct consultation
4. Determine Audit vs Workshop

### "Consultation went great, sending SOW"
1. Choose Audit or Workshop SOW template
2. Customize for their needs
3. Send for signature
4. Status: `Prospect - Sent SOW - Awaiting Signature` 🔵
5. Wait for signature

### "They signed the SOW!"
1. Status: `Client - SOW Signed - Action Required` 🟢
2. Generate invoice
3. Create Stripe payment link
4. Send to client
5. Update status: `Client - Sent Invoice - Awaiting Payment` 🔵

### "Payment received from Stripe"
1. Status auto-updates: `Client - Payment Received - Delivering [Audit/Workshop]` 🟠
2. Begin delivery work
3. Time block for completion
4. Deliver high-quality service

### "I delivered the audit/workshop"
1. Send all final materials
2. Status: `Client - [Audit/Workshop] Delivered - Awaiting Feedback` 🔵
3. Wait for client response

### "They have feedback/questions"
1. Status: `Client - Feedback Received - Action Required` 🟢
2. Address their feedback
3. Make revisions if reasonable
4. Send updated materials
5. Confirm satisfaction

### "Everything's done, they're happy"
1. Status: `Client - Engagement Complete - Archived` ⚪
2. Add success note
3. Consider nurture follow-up
4. Track for referrals/future work

## 📊 Energy Management Dashboard

### Filter: "What needs my attention TODAY?"
**Show only:** 🟢 Green statuses (Action Required)

You'll see:
- Prospects who completed forms (review intake)
- Prospects with SOW questions (answer questions)
- Clients who signed SOW (generate invoice)
- Clients who sent feedback (address feedback)

### Filter: "What am I waiting on?"
**Show only:** 🔵 Blue statuses (Awaiting Response/Payment/Signature)

You'll see:
- Prospects who haven't completed forms yet
- Prospects who haven't booked consultation yet
- Prospects who haven't signed SOW yet
- Clients who haven't paid invoice yet
- Clients who haven't sent feedback yet

### Filter: "What am I actively delivering?"
**Show only:** 🟠 Orange statuses (Delivering/Scheduled)

You'll see:
- Consultations scheduled (prep needed)
- Audits in progress (delivery happening)
- Workshops being delivered (active engagement)

### Filter: "What's complete?"
**Show only:** ⚪ Gray statuses (Complete - Archived)

You'll see:
- All closed engagements
- Success metrics
- Referral opportunities
