# Jamie AI Assistant - Complete User Manual

## 🤖 **Who is Jamie?**

Jamie is your AI content creation assistant for Empower Health Strategies. She helps you transform ideas into published content while maintaining your authentic voice and patient-first perspective.

### **Jamie's Personality:**
- **Supportive, not pushy** - She suggests, never forces
- **Voice-conscious** - Learns and protects your brand voice
- **Patient-focused** - Always considers the patient perspective
- **Strategic** - Balances empathy with business goals
- **Collaborative** - Works WITH you, not instead of you

### **Jamie's Philosophy:**
> "I'm here to reduce friction in your writing process while amplifying your unique expertise. You're the expert - I'm the assistant who helps you share that expertise more effectively."

## 📊 **What Jamie Can Do (Current Features)**

### **✅ IMPLEMENTED - Available Now**

#### **1. Brain Dump 🧠**
**What:** Voice-to-text idea capture with AI structuring

**When to use:**
- You have a messy idea in your head
- Walking/driving and want to capture thoughts
- Too many ideas at once
- Don't know where to start

**How it works:**
1. Click "Brain Dump" button
2. Speak your thoughts (no structure needed)
3. Jamie transcribes and organizes into:
   - Main topic
   - Key points
   - Potential angle
   - Suggested next steps

**Example:**
```
You say (messily):
"So I was thinking about prior auth and how it affects 
IBD patients, like there's this huge delay and they're 
in pain waiting and nobody talks about that emotional 
toll, maybe a LinkedIn post?"

Jamie structures:
Topic: Prior Authorization Impact on IBD Patients
Key Points:
- Treatment delays due to prior auth
- Physical pain during waiting period
- Emotional/psychological toll (underreported)
Angle: Patient perspective on system failures
Platform: LinkedIn Post (300-500 words)
```

**Pro tip:** Speak naturally - Jamie handles the messy parts.

#### **2. Generate Title Options ✍️**
**What:** AI-generated title suggestions based on your content

**When to use:**
- You have an idea but can't nail the title
- Your title feels boring
- You want multiple angle options

**How it works:**
1. Open content in editor
2. Jamie panel → "Generate Titles"
3. Get 3-5 title options
4. Click to use or inspire your own

**Title types:**
- **Question-based:** "Why Do Patients Struggle With [X]?"
- **Data-driven:** "The Hidden Cost of [X]"
- **Myth-busting:** "The Truth About [X] That Healthcare Leaders Miss"
- **Personal:** "What Patients Wish You Knew About [X]"
- **Listicle:** "5 Ways [X] Impacts Patient Experience"

**Pro tip:** Mix and match elements from different suggestions.

#### **3. Add Blueprint Template 📋**
**What:** Structured content templates for different content types

**When to use:**
- Starting a new piece
- Stuck on structure
- Want proven frameworks
- Need consistency across content

**Available Blueprints:**

**📖 Story Family:**
- **Insight Story** - Personal experience with strategic lesson
- **Patient Journey** - Following a patient's experience
- **Transformation Story** - Before/after narrative
- **Day in the Life** - Immersive perspective piece

**💭 Perspective Family:**
- **Hot Take** - Bold opinion with evidence
- **Myth vs Reality** - Debunk common misconceptions
- **What Patients Wish You Knew** - Patient advocacy angle
- **The [X] Nobody Talks About** - Underexplored topic

**📚 Education Family:**
- **Framework/Model** - Structured approach to a problem
- **Listicle** - Numbered tips/insights
- **How-To Guide** - Step-by-step instructions
- **Comparison** - Side-by-side analysis

**How it works:**
1. Jamie panel → "Add Blueprint"
2. Choose blueprint family
3. Select specific template
4. Jamie inserts structure with prompts
5. Fill in sections OR ask Jamie to generate

**Example - Insight Story Blueprint:**
```
## The Moment
[Describe specific incident/observation]

## The Context
[Why this matters/background]

## The Insight
[What you learned/realized]

## The Implication
[What this means for healthcare/patients]

## The Path Forward
[Actionable next step or reflection]
```

**Pro tip:** Blueprints are starting points - customize freely!

#### **6. Score/Rank Inbox Items 📥**
**What:** AI relevance scoring for content ideas from emails

**When to use:**
- Gmail integration pulls newsletter stories
- Too many ideas to process
- Need to prioritize what to write

**How Jamie scores (0-100):**
- **Relevance** to your expertise (40%)
- **Uniqueness** of angle (30%)
- **Timeliness** of topic (20%)
- **Audience interest** potential (10%)

**Score Guide:**
- **90-100:** Perfect fit - write this now
- **70-89:** Strong potential - add to pipeline
- **50-69:** Moderate fit - consider if passionate
- **Below 50:** Low priority - archive or skip

**Visual in Inbox:**
```
[95] "New research on patient portal adoption 
     barriers" ⭐⭐⭐
     → Highly relevant, unique angle, trending topic

[72] "Digital health trends for 2025"
     → Good fit but covered elsewhere

[45] "General wellness tips for holidays"
     → Off-brand, low uniqueness
```

**Pro tip:** Scores are guidance - your passion overrides the algorithm!

## 💡 **Jamie's Voice Guidelines**

Jamie is trained on these principles for Empower Health Strategies:

### **Patient-First Language:**
✅ "Patients deserve..." not "Healthcare systems should..."
✅ "Real people facing..." not "Population segments with..."
✅ "Sarah waited three weeks..." not "Average wait times are..."

### **Empathetic + Strategic:**
✅ Balance emotion with data
✅ Tell stories, then explain implications
✅ Show compassion AND business case

### **Authentic, Not Corporate:**
✅ "Here's what I learned..." not "Research indicates..."
✅ "This breaks my heart" not "This is suboptimal"
✅ Personal pronouns and vulnerability

### **Actionable Insight:**
✅ Every piece should have "so what?"
✅ Reader leaves with something to do/think/change
✅ Theory grounded in practice

## 🆘 **Troubleshooting**

### **"Jamie's suggestions don't sound like me"**
- Use "Suggest Improvements" to get feedback
- Edit more deliberately (Jamie learns from edits)
- Check if you're in the right blueprint
- Override and teach Jamie your preference

### **"Jamie keeps suggesting topics I'm not interested in"**
- Archive low-scored inbox items
- Your engagement patterns train the algorithm
- Manually adjust scores if needed

### **"I'm stuck and Jamie isn't helping"**
- Try Brain Dump to get thoughts out
- Switch to different blueprint
- Generate Introduction to just get started
- Step away and come back (sometimes you need space, not AI)

### **"Jamie gave me three options and they all feel wrong"**
- Perfect! Now you know what you DON'T want
- Use elements from each to create option 4
- Trust your creative instinct
