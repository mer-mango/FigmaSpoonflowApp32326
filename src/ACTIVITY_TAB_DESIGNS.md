# Activity Tab Design Options

## Option 1: Clean Minimal with Status Cards
**Layout**: Full-width timeline with subtle card design
- **Nurture Schedule Section**: Horizontal bar at top with dropdown, next due date, and "On Track" badge
- **Activity Cards**: White cards with subtle left border (teal for meetings, purple for nurtures)
- **Card Style**: Minimalist with collapsible sections using chevrons
- **Task Display**: Compact list with inline badges
- **Spacing**: Generous white space between cards
- **Visual Hierarchy**: Strong typography, minimal icons

---

## Option 2: Color-Coded Timeline
**Layout**: Vertical timeline with colored dots and connecting line
- **Nurture Schedule Section**: Card format at top with icon, similar to profile cards
- **Activity Cards**: Cards connected by vertical line on left side
- **Timeline Dots**: Color-coded dots (teal = meeting, purple = nurture, green = email)
- **Card Style**: Rounded with shadow on hover
- **Task Display**: Grid layout for tasks (2 columns)
- **Spacing**: Tight timeline feel
- **Visual Hierarchy**: Timeline creates strong visual flow

---

## Option 3: Split View with Filters
**Layout**: Two-column layout with filters on left
- **Left Sidebar (25%)**: Nurture schedule + filter checkboxes (Meetings, Nurtures, All)
- **Right Timeline (75%)**: Activity feed
- **Activity Cards**: Full-width cards with icon badges at top-right
- **Card Style**: Bordered cards with subtle background tint based on type
- **Task Display**: Expandable list with "Show all tasks" link
- **Spacing**: Medium density
- **Visual Hierarchy**: Sidebar creates clear organization

---

## Option 4: Compact List View
**Layout**: Dense list view for power users
- **Nurture Schedule Section**: Inline banner at top (not a card)
- **Activity Cards**: Compressed cards with all info visible
- **Card Style**: Alternating light gray background for visual separation
- **Expandable Sections**: None - all content always visible
- **Task Display**: Inline badges with count indicator
- **Spacing**: Minimal spacing (more items visible at once)
- **Visual Hierarchy**: Grid lines and alternating backgrounds

---

## Option 5: Card Carousel/Dashboard Style  
**Layout**: Grid view of activity cards
- **Nurture Schedule Section**: Large card at top spanning full width with chart/progress indicator
- **Activity Cards**: 2-column grid of activity cards
- **Card Style**: Elevated cards with strong shadows and rounded corners
- **Card Header**: Bold with type icon and date
- **Task Display**: Preview of 3 tasks with "View all" button
- **Spacing**: Card grid with gaps
- **Visual Hierarchy**: Dashboard feel with metrics

---

## My Recommendation: Option 1 (Clean Minimal with Status Cards)
**Why**: 
- Matches your existing minimal aesthetic (Akiflow/Reclaim.ai inspiration)
- Scales well with many activities
- Collapsible sections keep it clean
- Easy to scan chronologically
- Consistent with your current 2-column modal design
